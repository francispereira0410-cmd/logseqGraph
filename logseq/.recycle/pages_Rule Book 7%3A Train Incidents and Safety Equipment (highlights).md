summary:: Train operators must tell the controller, follow instructions, and keep customers informed or detrain them. Station staff and customer service managers must secure platforms, stop trains, switch off traction if needed, and help evacuate customers. Controllers must coordinate the response, send duty managers, preserve evidence, and move or withdraw the train for investigation.
