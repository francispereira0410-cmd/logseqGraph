summary:: This book sets out roles and procedures for managing railway incidents. Category two incidents are handled locally by the service manager with support from the LU senior operating officer. Category one incidents are declared by the LU senior operating officer, who leads formal incident management, appoints silver and bronze control, coordinates with emergency services, and arranges handover when resolved.
