summary:: The service manager decides the course of action and the controller must have authority before acting.  
All staff must agree the train location, limits of movement, protection method, and how any points will be secured.  
Train operators must wait for clear authorisation, follow special rules for signals at danger or single-line working, and proceed slowly with protection confirmed.
