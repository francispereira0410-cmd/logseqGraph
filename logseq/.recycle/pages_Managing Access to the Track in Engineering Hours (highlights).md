summary:: This guide sets out rules for safe track access during Engineering Hours. The track access controller must coordinate with controllers, signallers and the power control room and only issue Line Clear/Line Safe when all staff are clear or protected. It also covers late-surrender protection, cancelling or suspending Engineering Hours, locating missing staff, and managing extra trains.
