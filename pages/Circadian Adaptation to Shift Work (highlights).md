summary:: Shift work can disrupt the body's natural sleep-wake cycle, leading to sleep difficulties and health issues like fatigue, obesity, and mental health problems. Workers on rotating shifts often struggle more with sleep compared to those on fixed shifts. Understanding these risks and implementing strategies for better sleep can help mitigate the negative effects of shift work.
