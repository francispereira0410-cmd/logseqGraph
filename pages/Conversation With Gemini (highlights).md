	- Which one should you use?
	  
	  Since Logseq treats them the same under the hood, the choice is purely about **visual signal-to-noise ratio**.
	  
	  Most users follow the "Inline vs. Metadata" rule:
	  
	  1.  If the word is part of your sentence, use `[[ ]]`.
	    
	  2.  If you are adding a category or status to the end of a line, use `#` ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kjg95ztdzkecpvxvhsgtj9ny))
